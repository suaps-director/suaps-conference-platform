Opening Keynote: The Empirical Weird
Streaming via StreamYard → YouTube Live
Speaker
Dr. Michael Cifone
Founder & President, SUAPS · PhD Philosophy of Science, University of Maryland
Abstract
This keynote introduces the theoretical framework of the "empirical weird" as the central organising concept for UAP studies as a nascent interdisciplinary field — examining how phenomena at the threshold of scientific classifiability demand new epistemological tools drawn from philosophy, physics, anthropology, and the history of science.
